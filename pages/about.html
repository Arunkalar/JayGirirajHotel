About Jay Giriraj

A legacy of authentic flavors and warm hospitality

Our Story

Jay Giriraj Hotel & Restaurant has been serving authentic vegetarian cuisine for over two decades. Founded in 2000 with a vision to preserve traditional cooking methods while embracing modern hospitality standards, we have become a beloved destination for food enthusiasts and travelers alike.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that good food brings people together. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a full-service hotel and restaurant, but our commitment to quality, authenticity, and warm hospitality remains unchanged.

Every dish we serve is prepared with love, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. We source the freshest ingredients locally and prepare everything in-house to ensure the highest quality and authentic taste that our guests have come to expect.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Rajesh Kumar

With over 25 years of culinary experience, Chef Rajesh Kumar is the master behind our kitchen. Born and raised in a family of traditional cooks, he learned the art of vegetarian cooking from his grandmother and has been perfecting these recipes ever since.

Specialties:

  • Traditional North Indian Cuisine
  • Gujarati and Rajasthani Delicacies
  • Regional Street Food
  • Festive & Wedding Catering

Chef's Philosophy:

"Cooking is not just about ingredients and techniques; it's about creating memories and bringing joy to people's lives. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries a piece of our heart."
